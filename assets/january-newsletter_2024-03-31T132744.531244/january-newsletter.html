EDUCATION AND EMANCIPATION : Legacy of Savitri Bai Phule

School of Social Sciences

 

3 JANUARY, 2024 | MINI AUDITORIUM |2:30 PM - 4:30 PM
 

The Sociology and Political Science Annual Lecture on “Education and Emancipation: Legacy of Savitri Bai Phule” will celebrate the legacy of the first Dalit woman teacher of India who was dedicated and committed to the emancipation of women in the country. Phule faced several hardships in her fight for this cause in a caste-ridden and patriarchal society. The resource person for the event is  Dr. Swapnil Dhanraj, Associate Professor of Jindal Global Law School, O.P. Jindal Global University. The lecture will highlight the significance of education and emancipation.

Come and join the insightful guest lecture!
